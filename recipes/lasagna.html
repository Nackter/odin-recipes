Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish made of stacked layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese. It is generally one of the most tried and tested recipes in Italy and is one of the most popular dishes in Western countries.

All you need to cook a great lasagna is ground beef, tomato sauce, and cheese. And some love.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Cook some ground beef
  2. place the beef, pasta and sauce in a casserrole
  3. Add cheese and cook in the oven for 30 minutes

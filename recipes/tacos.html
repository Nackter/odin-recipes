Tacos

Description

This is a recipe for tacos. Tacos are scrumptious. They are loved by everyone. Perhaps not by dogs.

Cook some ground beef. Add onions, garlic, and taco seasoning. Add cheese. Voila!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook some ground beef
  2. Add onions, garlic, and taco seasoning
  3. Add cheese
  4. Place heavy cream on the table
  5. Season to taste
